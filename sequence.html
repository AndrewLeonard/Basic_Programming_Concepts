Sequence

A computer program is made up of lots and lots of individual instructions put together in the right order to make a program - we call this sequencing.

It is important to get the individual instructions in the right order, otherwise the program will not work.

 

Programming is not that much difference to cooking.

With cooking you need the right ingredients mixed in the correct order to get the food you want.

Let's look at the sequence required for making baked beans on toast.

It is easy to make baked beans on toast! It might be a simple meal to make, but the sequencing is quite complex, there are lots of different things that must be done.

What ingredients do you need to make beans on toast?

Bread
Butter
Can of Baked Beans

What equipment do you require?

Toaster
Saucepan
Source of heat
Knife
Fork
Tin opener
